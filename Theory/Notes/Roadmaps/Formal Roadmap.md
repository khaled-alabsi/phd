# PhD Roadmap: Anomalies in Production Processes & Multivariate Control Charts

## Year 1: Topic Refinement & Literature Review
- **Month 1-3:**
  - Refine research questions related to production process disturbances and time series anomalies
  - Literature review on statistical process control (SPC), multivariate control charts, and AI methods
  - Identify relationships between variables in production processes
- **Month 4-6:**
  - Explore existing models for anomaly detection and variable relationships
  - Develop a conceptual framework for analyzing variable interdependencies
- **Month 7-12:**
  - Write Chapter 1: Introduction and Chapter 2: Literature Review

## Year 2: Methodology & Data Collection
- **Month 13-15:**
  - Finalize the methodology for studying relationships between variables using multivariate control charts
  - Identify relevant production process datasets
- **Month 16-18:**
  - Collect and preprocess data (e.g., production data, time series)
  - Conduct preliminary analysis of variable relationships
- **Month 19-24:**
  - Write Chapter 3: Methodology (detailed explanation of SPC, multivariate control charts, and AI methods)

## Year 3: Data Analysis & Variable Relationship Modeling
- **Month 25-27:**
  - Analyze relationships between variables using control charts and AI methods
  - Apply multivariate methods to identify key disturbances in production processes
- **Month 28-30:**
  - Implement anomaly detection algorithms
  - Interpret how variable relationships affect production process stability
  - Write Chapter 4: Results and Discussion (present results of variable relationship analysis and model performance)
- **Month 31-36:**
  - Refine models based on feedback
  - Start writing conclusions and implications for production process optimization

## Year 4: Conclusion & Thesis Finalization
- **Month 37-39:**
  - Finalize findings related to relationships between variables and their impact on production process disturbances
  - Complete draft of thesis
  - Prepare for defense
- **Month 40-42:**
  - Revise based on feedback
  - Prepare thesis defense presentation
- **Month 43-48:**
  - Defend thesis
  - Make final revisions and submit

## Ongoing Tasks:
- Attend relevant conferences on AI, SPC, and production optimization
- Keep refining your literature review based on new publications
- Publish papers on key findings related to anomaly detection and variable relationships
